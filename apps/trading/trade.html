05 / DAILY TIMELINE

A Day in the Life of the Bot

NIGHT BEFORE
Chart Analysis
Draw 30-40 S/R levels on Nifty, BankNifty, FinNifty across 1m/5m/15m timeframes. Save as dated JSON.
NIGHT BEFORE
Configure Daily Settings
Set fund allocation per index, shortlist strike prices, set max trades before lock (default 2), max daily loss.
09:00 IST
Boot Engine
Run main.py → Kite auth → load instruments → load levels → connect WebSocket → subscribe ticks.
09:15 IST
Market Open — Engine Live
Tick data flows in → candle builder starts constructing 1m/5m/15m candles in real-time.
09:16+ IST
Signal Scanning Begins
Every candle close triggers level scan + pattern match. Valid signals go to risk check → order executor.
DURING SESSION
Position Management
Open positions monitored tick-by-tick (SL/target) and candle-by-candle (timeout countdown).
AFTER 2 TRADES
Auto-Lock Activates
Bot blocks new entries. Continues monitoring existing positions. User can manually unlock via [u] key.
15:15 IST
EOD Squareoff
All remaining open positions force-exited at market price. No exceptions.
15:30 IST
Reports & Shutdown
Generate EOD report: PnL, missed trades analysis, level performance. Save to DB. Graceful shutdown.
06 / RISK MANAGEMENT

Iron-Clad Rules

These rules are hardcoded into the engine. They cannot be bypassed by any signal, no matter how strong.

01
Buy Options Only
We ONLY buy CE or PE. Never sell/write options. Max loss = premium paid. Risk is always defined.
02
Auto-Lock After 2 Trades
Win, loss, or neutral — after 2 trades the engine locks. Manual unlock only. Discipline over greed.
03
One Position Per Index
Cannot stack trades. Max 1 open position on Nifty, 1 on BankNifty, 1 on FinNifty at any time.
04
7-10 Candle Timeout
If target not hit within 7-10 candles of the signal's timeframe, auto-exit at last candle's close.
05
Hard EOD @ 15:15
All positions force-squared 15 minutes before close. No overnight. Product = MIS always.
06
Market Orders Only
Scalping demands speed. All entries and exits are MARKET orders. No limit orders, no GTT.
07
Max Daily Loss Cap
Optional daily loss limit. If set and breached, engine auto-locks permanently for the day.
08
Kill Switch
Press [k] at any time → instantly exit ALL positions at market and halt the engine completely.
